Welcome to the CLIx Platform                    


CLIxPlatform is a Next Generation Digital Learning Platform powered by gstudio (a Node Description Framework (NDF) based knowledge representation software architecture). It integrates OpenAssessments for assessment questions and various other learning tools available open source and/or developed by CLIx.  The platform is a result of collaborative  work between teams at Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai, India; Homi Bhabha Centre for Science Education, TIFR, Mumbai, India; and, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, MA USA.
